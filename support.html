Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I report a bug?

A: Please use the contact form above or email us directly. Include as much detail as possible about the issue, including when it occurs and steps to reproduce it.

Q: How long does it take to get a response?

A: We typically respond within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent issues, please mark your email as urgent.

Q: Can I request a new feature?

A: Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Please use the contact form and select "Feature Request" as the subject.

Q: Do you provide refunds?

A: Refund requests should be made through the App Store or Google Play Store according to their respective refund policies.
